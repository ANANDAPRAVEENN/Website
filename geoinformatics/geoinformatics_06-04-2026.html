What we do

End-to-End Geospatial Services

From raw point cloud to analysis-ready products — we deliver precision at every stage of the geospatial workflow.

LiDAR Processing

Comprehensive LiDAR point cloud processing from strip adjustment through to final classified deliverables.

  • Strip adjustment & photo calibration
  • Bare earth & building classification
  • Powerline & hydro classification
  • Hydro-flattened DTM/DSM creation
  • Flood modelling & forest inventory

Photogrammetry

Precision aerial triangulation, 3D city modelling, and ortho-photo production for mapping applications.

  • Aerial triangulation
  • 3D city modelling
  • DEM/DTM generation
  • Planimetric feature extraction
  • Contour generation

GIS Services

Topographic, cadastral, and thematic mapping with geospatial database design and GPS survey.

  • Topographic & cadastral maps
  • Thematic map generation
  • GPS survey
  • Oil & gas pipeline GIS databases

Orthophoto Production

High-quality ortho generation, mosaicing, colour balancing, seamline editing and tile cutting services.

  • Ortho generation & mosaicing
  • Colour balancing
  • Seamline editing
  • Tile cutting & edge matching

Laser Scanning

3D modelling, BIM/MEP, reverse engineering and surface texturing using terrestrial and mobile laser scanning.

  • 3D modelling (AutoCAD, Revit)
  • BIM/MEP documentation
  • Reverse engineering
  • Surface texturing

Engineering & CAD

Engineering drawing conversion, drafting bureau services, and raster-to-digital data conversion across Civil, Structural, MEP and Architecture verticals.

About us

Pioneers in
Spatial Intelligence

Geoinformatics is a multidisciplinary professional geospatial technology services company with innovative offerings and strategic partnerships with industry leaders in the global space. Innovation is the guiding force for all our services.

We offer services ranging from application development to enterprise integration, consulting, data conversion and capture — including field survey and enterprise system integration projects undertaken with our strategic partners.

Geoinformatics follows a standard methodology for issue escalation, progress monitoring, quality reviews and status reporting. Tasks are planned with mutually agreed milestones, broken down into weekly work segments with regular tele-conference review meetings.
